Speak Loud, Stand Proud.

"Breaking one's silence using another."

What is our workshop all about?

Our workshop is made to help those who have a hard time in communication. We help those people to get used to talking to others and help them be more confident when speaking.

What do we have to offer?

We have a lot of activities in store for our workshop, we like to create an environment where people can be comfortable in their own space. Since it will not be easy on the first few minutes - knowing that not everyone knows each other. We have activities such as show & tell, partner activities and guest speakers to break the ice! There's a lot more but it's up to you if you wanna find out.
